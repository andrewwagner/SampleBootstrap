LOANS FOR A BETTER FUTURE

Oportun, formerly Progreso Financiero, helps customers with limited or no credit history build a better future by:

1

Offering personal loans of $300 - $6,000* with affordable, fixed payments (no bank account, previous loan, or collateral like auto titles required).

2

Helping individuals establish or build their credit history by reporting customer accounts to credit bureaus.

3

Providing personal service through bilingual (English/Spanish) staff in 170+ locations in California, Illinois, Nevada, Texas, and Utah in addition to bilingual staff at call centers.

Oportun was founded in 2005 with the goal of providing credit-building, affordable loans to the approximately 25 million financially-underserved Hispanics in the US, many of whom have limited or no credit history.

To learn more about Oportun, including how the company has been able to leverage technology and advanced data analytics to score the “unscoreable” and provide affordable, credit-building loans to hundreds of thousands of customers, please visit “About Us.”
